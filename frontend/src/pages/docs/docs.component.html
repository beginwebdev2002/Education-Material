How to Use EduGen

Welcome to the documentation for the Education Material Generator. This guide will walk you through the simple process of creating your first set of materials.

Step 1: Navigate to the Dashboard

The heart of our application is the Dashboard. You can access it from the main navigation bar at the top of the page. This is where all the magic happens.

Step 2: Fill in the Generation Form

The form is designed to be intuitive. You'll need to provide three key pieces of information to get started:

  • Subject Name: The name of the course or subject (e.g., "World History 101").
  • Target Audience: Who the material is for (e.g., "High School Sophomores").
  • Topic/Module Description: This is the most important part. Provide a detailed description of the topic, including learning objectives, key concepts, and any specific points you want to be covered. The more detail you provide, the better the generated content will be.

Step 3: Upload a Template (Optional)

If you have a specific format or branding you need to follow, you can upload a DOCX or PDF file as a template. Our AI will do its best to match the style and layout of your provided template.

Step 4: Select Your Desired Outputs

Choose one or more output formats from the available options:

  • Рабочая программа (Work Program): A detailed syllabus and curriculum outline.
  • Лекция 12(Lecture): A complete lecture script on the topic.
  • Презентация (Presentation): Key points formatted for a slide presentation.
  • Тест (Test): A set of questions to assess understanding of the material.

Step 5: Generate and Wait

Click the "Generate Material" button. The process can take a moment as our AI works to craft your content. A loading indicator will show that the system is working. Once complete, you'll receive a notification and links to download your new materials.

That's it! You're now ready to create amazing educational content with ease.
